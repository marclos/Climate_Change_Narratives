Impacts

  • Missouris climate is changing.

  • Most of the state has warmed one-half to one degree (F) in the last century, and floods are becoming more frequent.

  • In the coming decades, the state will have more extremely hot days, which may harm public health in urban areas and corn harvests in rural areas.
