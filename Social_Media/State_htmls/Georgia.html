Impacts

  • In the coming decades, Georgia will become warmer, and the state will probably experience more severe floods and drought.

  • Recent trends suggest more rain is falling in heavy downpours, and sea level is rising about one inch every decade.

  • Higher water levels are eroding beaches, submerging low lands, and exacerbating coastal flooding.
